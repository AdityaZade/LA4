About us

In any school, it's too hard to maintain library since they have students from different braches and traits , if students wants to take any particular books from library, it's very hard to search manually. To solve this, Library Management System has been developed and designed that allows access to the library wherein the user can view availability of the books and also keep a check on the stock. The automated library system has been created with the intention of making libraries more organized and to make library stocking and accessing more simplified and structured. It uses technology in terms of online stock checking, procurement, check-in and check-out, fine management and the status of a book. It has become an essential tool for large libraries which have a huge number of books since stock checking and procurement is a lot simpler using the various technological options and a structured approach
